How to use this app

This app helps you track your progress in completing your social care induction framework if you’re away from the office or out and about. It does not allow you to complete the detail of your induction, but you can check on what you need to do and tick those sections you’ve already done. You can then save the record of your progress as a PDF and e-mail it to yourself or your manager.

To see the full version of the social care induction framework Wales as a web or word document www.ccwales.org.uk/induction-frameworks/
