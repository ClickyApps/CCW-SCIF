A guide to using the progress log...

The SCIF has six common learning objectives for new workers in social care. There is also learning objective 7 for those working with children and young people and learning objective 8 for those working with adults. Learning objective 7 or 8 should be completed in addition to the six common learning objectives.

Please note that induction outcomes that are workplace-specific have been highlighted in bold.
