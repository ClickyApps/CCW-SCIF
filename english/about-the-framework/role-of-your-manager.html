Role of your manager

  • Your manager has a responsibility to ensure that you are given enough time, encouragement and support to reach successful completion and truly learn from the induction process.

    • Self-assessment
    • Portfolios

    • Job-specific tests
    • Simulations / role play
    • Other learning programmes
    • Written or oral reflection
