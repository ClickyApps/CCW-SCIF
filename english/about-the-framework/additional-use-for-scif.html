About the Framework

Additional use for SCIF

  • The SCIF can be used as a basis for agreeing and recording any future learning development activities and other further essential learning still required at induction for specific tasks not covered by the SCIF. This should support your continuing professional development (CPD).

  • As the SCIF has been designed and mapped to the mandatory units within the Qualification and Credit Framework (QCF) Diplomas, The Progress Log enables you to gather and record evidence that can be used to enable progress towards gaining qualifications.
